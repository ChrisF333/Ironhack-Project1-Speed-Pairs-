How to play

Speed pairs was made for stupid people like... oh nevermind. It's very simple: just find 12 pairs of cards before the time runs out. A pair can be, for example, any two eights of any suit. To match two cards, simply click on one and then on the other - if your choices match they will be removed from the board. But you better be quick, the timer starts as soon as you start the game! Such fun.


One last thing: watch out for the joker. If you click on him you lose instantly!
